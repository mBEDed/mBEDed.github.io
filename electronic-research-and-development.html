Embedded Systems Development

Our R&D expertise in embedded systems focuses on designing and optimizing hardware and firmware for dedicated functions. From microcontrollers to real-time operating systems, we develop robust and efficient solutions for various applications, including industrial control, consumer electronics, and automotive systems.

IoT & Connectivity Solutions

We provide R&D services for the Internet of Things, encompassing sensor integration, wireless communication protocols (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, Cellular), edge computing, and cloud connectivity. Our goal is to create smart, connected devices that gather data, enable remote control, and enhance operational efficiency.

Advanced Power Electronics

Our R&D in power electronics focuses on developing efficient and reliable power conversion and management solutions. This includes designing AC/DC, DC/DC converters, battery management systems, and motor drives for applications in renewable energy, electric vehicles, and industrial power supplies.

Analog & Mixed-Signal Design

We specialize in R&D for analog and mixed-signal circuits, crucial for processing real-world signals. Our services include sensor interfacing, signal conditioning, data acquisition, and high-precision measurement systems, ensuring accuracy and performance in complex electronic designs.

AI & Machine Learning Design

Our R&D services for AI and machine learning focus on developing intelligent algorithms, machine learning models, and deep learning frameworks. We create innovative solutions for image and speech recognition, natural language processing, and autonomous systems, ensuring your products stay ahead of the competition.
