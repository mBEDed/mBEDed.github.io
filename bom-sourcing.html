Smart Component Sourcing


In today's volatile market, a robust supply chain is as critical as the design itself. We move beyond simple procurement to offer comprehensive lifecycle management. Our team proactively monitors component lifecycles, mitigates supply risks, and leverages a global network of authorized distributors to ensure your production lines never stop.

Direct sourcing from authorized distributors and verified manufacturers to guarantee authenticity and traceability.

Proactive monitoring of EOL (End-of-Life) notices with Last Time Buy (LTB) execution and drop-in replacement identification.

Strategic BOM analysis to identify cost-saving opportunities through cross-referencing and volume aggregation.

Rapid response sourcing for hard-to-find parts using our vetted global broker network with rigorous quality testing.

Reserved inventory for our clients' critical components to ensure uninterrupted production.

0 Tolerance QC Process

We understand the risks of the open market. Our incoming inspection process is rigorous and standardized.

  • Visual Inspection: Microscopy for lead condition, marking consistency, and package integrity.
  • Traceability: Full lot and date code tracking for every component.
  • Testing: Electrical verification and decapsulation for high-risk components.
