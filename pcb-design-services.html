Professional PCB Design Services



We provide end-to-end PCB design services backed by a team of highly skilled designers, each with over 10 years of experience in high-speed digital, analog, mixed-signal, and RF systems. Our capabilities cover single-sided, double-sided, and complex multilayer boards (up to 32 layers), including rigid, IMS/metal-core, flexible, and rigid-flex PCBs. We support advanced manufacturing techniques such as thru-hole, blind and buried vias, micro-vias, and back-drilling.


Whether your project demands high-speed, constraint-driven routing or straightforward I/O layouts, our engineers have the expertise to deliver precise, well-managed designs that meet the highest quality standards and are completed on time.

Accurate and optimized PCB layouts created directly from your provided circuit schematics.

Expert single-ended, differential, and hatch-type impedance-controlled routing for high-speed performance.

Specialized PCB designs optimized for RF circuits, signal clarity, and minimal loss.

Designs incorporating BGAs, micro-vias, blind and buried vias for complex high-speed interconnects.

Routing executed to exact trace/space, keep-out, and clearance specifications provided by the customer.

Reproduce or rebuild existing PCBs or PCBAs from scratch with full documentation.

Logical component rearrangement for better physical layout with accurate updates back to the schematic.

Design of PCBs in irregular or unconventional shapes to meet unique mechanical requirements.
