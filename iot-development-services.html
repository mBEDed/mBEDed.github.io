Accelerating Innovation Through

IoT Research & Development Services

At mBEDed Systems we turn connected ideas into production-ready IoT products. As an Electronics Systems & Design Manufacturer (ESDM), we combine embedded hardware, firmware, cloud and data expertise to design, prototype and scale secure, reliable IoT solutions — from smart devices and gateways to edge/cloud platforms and analytics.

Quick overview of our IoT R&D Services

  • End-to-end IoT R&D — concept → prototype → deployment → scale
  • Device engineering — hardware, embedded firmware, sensors & connectivity
  • Cloud & edge integration — secure telemetry, APIs, and platform services
  • Data & AI — real-time analytics, ML at the edge, predictive insights
  • Security-first — device hardening, encryption, secure OTA updates
  • Deployment & scale — pilot runs, fleet management, lifecycle support

How it all Works?

We follow a pragmatic, repeatable IoT R&D flow: start with discovery and feasibility to define use-cases, connectivity and success criteria; then design hardware and embedded firmware in parallel with cloud/edge architecture. Rapid prototypes and PoCs validate end-to-end functionality. Once validated, we harden security, run thorough testing (connectivity, performance, environmental) and prepare compliance documentation. Pilot deployments prove the solution in the field while we optimize for manufacturability, OTA, device management and data operations — then scale to full production and provide lifecycle support for firmware, components and analytics.


Key Aspects of Our IoT R&D Services

  • Concept, Feasibility & PoC We validate ideas with feasibility studies and proof-of-concepts that define system architecture, connectivity options, power budget and costs — reducing technical risk before design begins.
  • Hardware & Embedded System Design Full-stack device engineering: sensor selection, schematic capture, multi-layer PCB layout, power management and radio design (BLE, Wi-Fi, LoRa, LTE). Firmware includes BSP, low-power strategies, secure boot and OTA.
  • Connectivity & Edge Integration Design and implement robust device-to-edge and device-to-cloud connectivity, MQTT/HTTP stacks, gateway logic, local processing and edge computing to meet latency and availability goals.
  • Cloud Platforms & Backend Services Cloud architecture, device management, scalable APIs, data pipelines and integrations with AWS/Azure/GCP or private platforms — designed for telemetry, remote management and analytics.
  • Data Engineering & AI/Analytics Real-time streaming, data aggregation, dashboards and ML models for anomaly detection, predictive maintenance and business intelligence. We optimize models for cloud or on-device inference.
  • Security, Privacy & OTA Secure elements, encryption, authentication, secure provisioning and end-to-end data protection. We build OTA update systems and lifecycle security practices for long-term device fleets.
  • Testing, Validation & Compliance Connectivity & interoperability testing, power and performance profiling, environmental and regulatory checks, and functional validation to ensure field readiness.
  • Pilot Deployments & Scale-Up Field trials, fleet onboarding, device provisioning, telematics dashboards and operational support — followed by manufacturing handoff, BOM optimization and supply-chain planning for scale.
