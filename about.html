About Us

The Start-Up Story

Even if you don’t have a perfect idea, to begin with, you can likely adapt.

While being a native of the Republic of India, We also felt like this is not a homeland for embedded systems. And all the engineers from the field were leaving for software industries, higher studies, or abroad.

We founded mBEDed µSystems because we needed it in our own life. The start-up is Research and Development Firm involved in ranging from sketching out an idea to carving out a full-fledged product.

We constantly innovate our products and ideas for our clients. mBEDed µSystems standardizes pricing to the whole RnD process to protect clients from unforeseen bumps or failures while giving service in a wider array of products with all the emerging technologies to the clients. When you move through life fast as today, a little bit of disorganization is bound to happen. And we are experts in rattling through all those situations.

That’s a win-win for everyone!
