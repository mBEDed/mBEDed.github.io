Electronic Product Design Services

At mBEDed Systems we design and build market-ready electronic products, from system architecture and PCB design to firmware, prototyping and low-to-high-volume manufacturing. As an Electronics Systems & Design Manufacturer (ESDM), we focus on real product development: reliable hardware, embedded software, compliance, and manufacturability.

Design, Build, Manufacture: Full-Stack Electronic Product Design Service

  • Full product development lifecycle: concept → prototype → production
  • ESDM expertise: system-level design, PCB, firmware, enclosure & test
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM): cost- and scale-optimized designs
  • Industry knowledge: medical, industrial, automation, security & IoT
  • Flexible delivery: in-house engineering, outsourced engagement, or partner model

Our mission is simple: to help you accelerate product development while reducing risks, costs, and time-to-market.


Key Features of our product design services

  • System & Electronic Design: End-to-end electrical architecture as in schematic capture, multi-layer PCB layout, power management, RF and high-speed design. We create designs that meet performance, EMC and thermal requirements for production-ready products.
  • Embedded Firmware & Software: Firmware development for microcontrollers, BSPs, device drivers, RTOS integration, secure boot and OTA/cloud connectivity. We deliver stable embedded software that ties hardware to services and UX.
  • Mechanical & Enclosure Integration: Collaborate on enclosure, thermal, and mounting design to ensure reliable fit, manufacturability and user ergonomics. We validate mechanical integration with PCB placement, connectors and assembly constraints.
  • Prototyping, Test & Validation: Rapid prototypes, functional test rigs, board bring-up and lab validation (EMC/pre-compliance, environmental testing). Early testing reduces risk and accelerates iteration toward a robust product.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Support (Medical, Safety): Design and documentation aligned to regulatory frameworks (ISO 13485, FDA guidance, industry safety standards) for medical, industrial and life-science products. We prepare the technical files and verification artifacts required for approvals.
  • Industrial & Automation Systems: Control systems, sensor integration, PLC/edge devices and IoT gateways engineered for reliability in harsh environments. Focus on deterministic control, secure communications and long lifecycle support.
  • Security & Surveillance Product Design: Secure hardware and firmware for access control, cameras and IoT security modules — including secure element integration, encrypted communications and tamper-resistant design.
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM) & Production Support: BOM optimization, component selection, panelization, test strategy and pilot runs to scale from prototypes to mass production while minimizing cost and yield issues.

How it Works ?

Our product development follows a clear, repeatable path that turns concepts into production-ready electronics. We start with discovery and requirements to capture use-cases, performance targets and regulatory constraints, then validate feasibility and define system architecture (MCU, RF, power and cloud integration). Next we complete detailed electrical and mechanical design — schematics, multi-layer PCB layout, signal integrity, thermal and enclosure integration — alongside embedded firmware development (BSP, drivers, RTOS, secure boot and connectivity). Rapid prototyping and board bring-up let us iterate quickly, while test, validation and compliance preparation (EMC, environmental, documentation) reduce risk. Finally, we optimize for manufacturing with BOM/DFM work, pilot runs and production transfer, then provide lifecycle support for components, firmware updates and quality management to ensure smooth scaling to volume production.
